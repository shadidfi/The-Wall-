Delirium

-Rakin (3011)

Thou ruined my being,
Thou art ruination.
Thou destroyed me, darling,
Thou art destruction.
Thou who shone,
Never yet always, like a sequin,
For debt of thine heart,
Mine thou sequestrated,
Now through my destruction and ruin,
Thou, not the others, shall be devastated.

For thy mother and my father,
For my sister and thy brother,
For the illiterate and the innocents,
For the literate and the delinquents;
For everyone, all, me and thee
I will hex,
And slowly stop that heart of mine,
I will carefully burn,
And slowly stop this heart of thine.

For those I talked of
And those I didn’t to be free,
For them all, you need fall,
For us, need fall we,
In that the abyss mightn’t do us apart,
In that even for a tick in life, we shan’t part;
While end of me not even ghostly I rue,
How the end of thee gives me a regret anew!
As it harshly tears at my cursed heart.

Always was I rational-
But now,
Whilst most in thee see repulsion,
I in thee see beauty,
Whilst some in thee see danger,
I in thee see safety.
As such, now thou art my delusion-
Hence, I am to be your messenger,
Hence, I am to be your brevity
With rational intentions, making a rash decision.

As I give this message,
Know that The Fates never play around,
And stench of poison and smell of love diffuse.
As I speak of the strings-
The pieces of strings laying about o the ground,
Words of love and death effuse.
All in pair for love is abound,
Abundant in its own right,
Enough for fearing the darkness without the others light.
And thus, though thou know now all,
Thou won’t refuse me,
For in this world and our own,
This is us, this is we.
As such, This loving poisonous kiss,
Shall set our heart and stomach ablaze,
And all is fine and all is amiss,
As eternal night starts, ends our days.
Myself for loved,
Thyself for unloved,
Unambiguously, with and without craze,
For this world shall rise, only whilst we raze.
