Ode to my hearts

-Mosfiq (2981)

Thou pleaded to be my muse
Oh my sweetheart!
Thou pleaded to not be her’s
Oh my heart!
I declined most my values
And accepted both thy requests.

While my heart
Thou are mine,
Utterly confused, I ask
Am I thine?

And my heart?
Must I remain a mystery to thee?
Or maybe, from within I hear murmurs
Have I lost thee?
Without my or thy knowledge,
While in me beats her’s,
And thou, in privilege
Resonate inside her, harmonious with her’s?

Must I remain such to myself, my hearts?
Writing hymns and odes
In both thy names,
Still for me and still for thee?

Oh my hearts! Oh my muses!
Have I lost the ability
To differentiate thee apart?
Can I really not,
Accept both thy request?

Oh but now I know,
All I need do, is let go-
Let go of all the beautiful emotions,
And embrace all my values,
And that way, accept both thy requests.
